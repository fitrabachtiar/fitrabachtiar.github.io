# Research Interest

I have interest in the following fields:

Using physiological signal and human preceptions to understand human's affect using computational methods.

Analyzing physiological signal and human preceptions to seacrh for something more meaningful.

Data Mining

Utilizing data mining techniques to extract knowledge and exploring new ways in conducting the analysis.

Mining student data during learning especially related to the human psychology and pyshiological signal to better understand student learning.

Discovery and analysing pattern in a data with focus on affect, psychology, pyshiological signal, and various field in general.
